Beginning therapy - whether for the first time or starting with someone new - can feel like a big step. It’s common to feel unsure about where to start, or whether talking to someone will help.

 

People often reach out when things begin to feel difficult to manage alone, whether that’s anxiety, relationship strain, or the pressures of balancing work, family life, and a sense of yourself.

 

What matters most is finding someone you feel comfortable enough with. A space where you can speak openly, at your own pace, and feel listened to without judgement.

 

I offer a free 15-minute introductory call, which gives you the chance to ask any questions and get a sense of whether working together feels like the right fit.

 

If you’d like to get in touch, you’re very welcome to complete the contact form below or send me an email. There’s no pressure to commit, it’s simply an opportunity to make an initial connection and see how it feels.
